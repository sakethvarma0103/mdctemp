Meta Developer Circles are a group of developers and tech enthusiasts who come together to learn, share, and network with one another. The idea behind these circles is to provide a platform for individuals to grow their skills and knowledge, as well as connect with like-minded people in the tech industry.

Machine Learning

MDC GITAM Visakhapatnam is bringing you Decoding ML, a two-day workshop based on concepts of Machine Learning

  • This workshop can assist you in learning new concepts and improving your fundamentals in Machine Learning.

  • This will be beneficial to any student with an interest in this field.

  • Gain hands-on experience with some Machine Learning projects in addition to a great learning experience.

  • Towards the end of the workshop, a quiz will be conducted based on what you learned from the workshop.

  • The winner will be awarded with gifts, and the attendees will also receive participation certificates!🔖

Creative Thinking

MDC GITAM Visakhapatnam is here with Apni Soch, an event aimed at encouraging digital artists and writers.

  • Apni Soch enables the artist/writer to express their "Soch" or point of view in the most creative way possible📝🎨

  • Allows the artist to highlight their imagination and creative side while also drawing viewers' attention to their concept👀

  • The topics are : Concepts of visualization of Metaverse, Open Source, Evolution of Art and Future of the World (Digital Art and Writers to pick any one of these)
